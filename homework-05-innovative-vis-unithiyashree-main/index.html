Homework #5: Innovative Visualization Design

Soviet Space Dogs

48 dogs who participated in the Soviet space program in the 1950s and 1960s.

The visualization shows the movement of rockets in a selected year with the number of dogs travelling along it. As you hover over each element, the details corresponding to them are displayed. If you click on the circle, the right part shows of the trends of the dog travelling in different rockets in different years.
