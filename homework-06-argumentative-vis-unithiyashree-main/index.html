Introduction :

I selected the TV shows dataset for the Argumentative visualization homework. This dataset has information about the ratings given for differnt tv shows present in OTT. The attributes present in the dataset are Title of the Tv Show, Year in which it is released, Target age group, IMDb Rating, Rotten Tomatoes Rating and OTT in which it is present amond Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hulu and Hotstar+. Since there is lot of data, I'm filtering the data using the Year selected by the user. For the visualizations, Im using the Imdb Rating and Rotten Tomatoes as the attributes to show the contrast.

Left Plot

The left plot shows the IMDb Ratings of the tv shows in the selected year. On hovering over the left bar graph, the name of the tv show, year in which it is released, IMDb Rating and OTT platform it is launched is shown.
The left plot used the Data provenance technique and Omission technique to show the IMDb data from the data source.

Right Plot

The right plot shows the Rotten Tomatoes Ratings of the tv shows in the selected year. On hovering over the right bar graph, the name of the tv show, year in which it is released, Rotten Tomatoes Rating and OTT platform it is launched is shown.
For the right plot, I used the rhetoric technique of Omission to create opposite narrative. Using omission, I removed the data which shows the same ratings of the tv shows on IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes.
